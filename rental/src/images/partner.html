To harness the power of technology in transforming lives and businesses across the African continent. We are dedicated to achieving this through our Kodex Academy, Kodex Build & the SpaceNg.

We've Got All the Skills You Need

Helping Africa's Bright Tech Minds Thrive

Here at Kodex Africa, we have a crucial role in supporting Africa's tech-savvy talents. We offer a wide range of programs aimed at nurturing valuable, long-lasting skills. Through scholarships and financial assistance, we make sure that financial challenges don't stand in the way of quality education. We focus on skill-building, offering coding bootcamps and workshops that prepare aspiring tech professionals with highly sought-after expertise.
Mentorship is at the heart of our approach, connecting emerging tech talents with experienced professionals who provide guidance and networking opportunities.
In summary, our versatile strategy encompasses education, skill development, mentorship, and entrepreneurship. We empower Africa's tech enthusiasts to thrive, innovate, and cultivate skills that stand the test of time. This, in turn, contributes to sustainable development and enhances Africa's influence in the global tech community.

Support Us

Support the Mission

Kodex Africa is on the mission of empowering the bright minds in Africa and drive technological innovation across the continent. Your support is essential in helping us achieve this goal and create a lasting impact. Your financial contributions directly assist our initiatives, which include providing scholarships and funding tech education programs. Every donation, regardless of its size, plays a significant role in advancing our mission.
You can also consider volunteering your time and expertise. Your skills can make a meaningful difference by mentoring aspiring tech professionals, participating in educational workshops, or assisting with our various projects. If you represent an organization or business, you can explore partnership opportunities with Kodex Africa. Collaborative efforts have the potential to expand our reach and generate innovative solutions to tech challenges in Africa.
Become an advocate for tech education and innovation in Africa. Spread the word about our mission, engage with policymakers, and help raise awareness about the importance of supporting tech talent in the region. Additionally, you can contribute by donating tech equipment or educational materials to enhance our educational programs and resources.
By supporting Kodex Africa, you are making an investment in the future of Africa's tech industry and contributing to sustainable development. Join us in empowering the next generation of tech leaders and innovators. Together, we can shape a brighter future for Africa's tech ecosystem.
