Digital Solutions

Digital Solutions - IT service provider in Switzerland

Technological innovation company helping companies with their digital transformation.

We can help you with all your IT services needs in Switzerland. Discover all our IT solutions and our team of top-flight engineers specialized in different technologies, to considerably increase productivity and the creation of high value-added software solutions.

We operate in all sectors, from banking, finance and insurance to institutions and transport. We also specialize in supporting Swiss watchmakers.

Our areas of invention also include the Public Sector and Swiss Cantons, Pharma and Life Sciences. Our IT solutions are also precisely tailored to the industrial, construction, retail and commercial sectors.

We can help you with all your IT services needs, innovation requests, IT security issues, system integration, web software migration or IT consulting.

We support you in software development, application security, third-party application maintenance and software industrialization. Digital Solutions aims to become one of the leading Swiss IT service providers in the canton of Jura and throughout Switzerland.

We can find the right engineers for your needs and the technologies you want to use: IT services, IT profiles (developers, project managers, project owners, DBAs, etc.), systems integration, business solutions, infrastructure management, business process services, cyber security, block chain, digital transformation.
